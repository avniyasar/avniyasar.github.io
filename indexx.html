About Me



I took my Bachelor's Degree from Mechanical Engineering, Middle East Technical University.

I worked as Design Engineer for 3 years in a private company in Ankara, Turkey.

I am a data scientist with 3 years of experience in developing machine-learning-based solutions to augment physical simulations, which enables accurate, efficient, robust, and optimum data driven design.

I am currently a Master's Student in Computational and Applied Mathematics at Middle East Technical University, Turkey.



I am experienced in machine learning, supervised and unsupervised algorithms and use data visualization techniques to present the results.

I have experience working with Deep Learning frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch) for my data science projects.



During my master studies ,I got hands-on experience with large datasets (e.g. ImageNet) and deep models (e.g. Resnet50). I have implemented state-of-the-art models from research paper.

Encouraged by witnessing how my solutions make the design process more intelligent and reliable, I am eager to apply my expertise to make such an impact on a much larger scale.
