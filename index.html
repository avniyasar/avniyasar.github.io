Physics Informed Neural Networks for Enhanced Critical Heat Flux Prediction in Hypersonic Flows

AIAA Aviation Forum · July 2024 — H. A. Yaşar, O. K. Sevinc

Abstract
We embed governing-equation terms into a PINN formulation to predict critical heat-flux on an axisymmetric blunt body across Mach/altitude conditions, achieving accuracy comparable to CFD with markedly reduced runtime.

Rapid Stagnation Heat Flux Prediction on Parametric Nose Cones in Hypersonic Flow via Variational Autoencoding

EUCASS · July 2025 — H. A. Yaşar, O. K. Sevinc

Abstract
A VAE encodes 2D nose-cone geometries to a compact latent; a conditional MLP maps latent + flight conditions to heat-flux, delivering fast inference on thousands of cases with strong generalization to holdout geometries.
